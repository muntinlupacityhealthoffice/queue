ABOUT

The City Health Office (CHO) was created under the Republic act No. 7160 also known as the Local Government Code where in public health service was one of the national functions devolved to local government units. It is the one in charge in the delivery of health services to the community and oversees the implementation of different health programs of the Department of Health (DOH).

The CHO in its own building is located at Centennial Avenue, Tunasan, Muntinlupa City. It has 15 health centers (HC) and one health station distributed in the 9 barangays of the city and is instituted depending on the population of the barangay.

The CHO generally manage the personnel and staff of the said office; it also coordinates with other government agencies and non-government agencies to ensure delivery of basic health care and promotion of health to the community. It also recommends to the Sangguniang Panglungsod through the Local Health Board an ordinance that will aid in the preservation and improvement of the public health.

SERVICES

Issuance and release of Death certificates, Transfer certificates or Authorization of exhumation remains

The City Health Office handles the issuance of death and transfer certificates, as well as exhumation permits, which are signed by the government physician.

Water Laboratory Services

The City Health Office operates a water laboratory that functions as a testing center to determine whether water is safe for human consumption, particularly for use in food establishments.

General Consultation and Treatment Services

he CHO is responsible for delivering health services to the community and implementing various Department of Health (DOH) programs. These include: 1. National Tuberculosis Program 2. National Immunization Program 3. Maternal, Neonatal, Child Health and Nutrition Program 4. Rabies Program 5. Dental Program 6. Maternal and Child Health Program 7. Family Planning, Reproductive Health, and STI Program 8. Nutrition Program 9. Non-Communicable Disease Program All these services are available in health centers across the barangays.

Issuance of medical Certificate required for newly hired and promotion

The City Health Office issues and signs medical certificates from the Civil Service Commission for newly hired and promoted employees.

Issuance of Sanitary Permit

One of the functions of the City Health Office is to issue Sanitary Permit as one of the requirements on the Sanitation Code.

Basic Laboratory Services

The City Health Office provides basic laboratories in health centers, serving as community-based facilities that deliver essential public health services.

Basic X-ray Services

The City Health Office offers basic X-ray services in health centers, providing community-based and patient-focused public health care.
